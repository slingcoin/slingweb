Sling is an open source project designed from the ground up to build out its own ecosystem.   • Why Make One?

    After PoW is finished, running a master node is an effective, easy and secure way of earning more Sling. Running a Sling master node gives you back a 67% cut of the staking coins on the entire Sling network.

  • How does it work?

    Start Slingd on the VPS. Deposit exactly 7331 Sling into the Sling address created for your node in cold wallet. After 15 confirmations, you may click start to start your slingshot node. Then sit back and collect payments from the network.
